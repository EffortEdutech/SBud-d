AI Study Buddy
Volume H12 – Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience

Version: 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

1. Introduction

The Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience defines how students manage their personal identity, subscription plan, preferences, and privacy controls.

This layer supports the long-term relationship between the student and AI Study Buddy.

The purpose is to provide:

Control.

Transparency.

Personalisation.

Trust.

2. Profile Experience Philosophy

The profile experience follows these principles.

Student Ownership

The student's:

Learning history.

PLKG.

Preferences.

Progress.

belong to the student.

Simple Management

Settings should be understandable and easy to control.

Personalisation

Students can configure how AI Study Buddy supports them.

Trust and Transparency

Students understand:

What data exists.

How it is used.

How they control it.

3. Profile Information Architecture

Structure:

Profile

├── Personal Information

├── Academic Profile

├── Learning Preferences

├── BLIE Preferences

├── Subscription

├── Privacy & Security

├── Data Management

└── Help & Support
4. Personal Profile

Students manage basic information.

Includes:

Name.

Profile image (optional).

Preferred language.

Communication preferences.

The profile represents the student identity within the learning environment.

5. Academic Profile

Academic information connects to the student's learning journey.

Includes:

University.

Programme.

Current semester.

Academic goals.

Academic information powers personalisation.

6. Learning Preferences

Students can configure how they learn.

Examples:

Explanation Style

Simple explanation.

Detailed explanation.

Example-based explanation.

Step-by-step guidance.

Learning Pace

Quick review.

Normal learning.

Deep exploration.

Study Preference

Reading.

Visual explanation.

Practice-based learning.

7. BLIE Personalisation Settings

Students control their AI companion experience.

Possible settings:

Response Style

Concise.

Balanced.

Detailed.

Learning Support Level

Reactive assistance.

Guided learning.

Proactive recommendations.

Reminder Preferences

Students control learning notifications.

8. Subscription Experience

AI Study Buddy follows a direct-to-student subscription model.

The platform is designed for individual students downloading the application from app stores.

9. Subscription Models

Initial structure:

Individual Subscription

Personal AI Study Buddy.

Includes:

Personal BLIE access.

Personal PLKG.

Learning analytics.

Document intelligence.

Group Subscription

Designed for students learning together.

Benefits:

Lower cost per student.

Shared study resources.

Collaborative learning environment.

10. Subscription Management

Students can manage:

Current plan.

Upgrade.

Renewal.

Billing history.

Subscription status.

The experience should be simple and transparent.

11. Privacy Controls

Students control their data.

Settings include:

Data visibility.

Sharing permissions.

Study group sharing.

AI memory preferences.

12. BLIE Memory Control

Students can manage AI memory.

Possible controls:

View remembered preferences.

Remove saved information.

Adjust memory behaviour.

The student remains in control.

13. Data Management

Students can manage their learning data.

Options:

Export learning history.

Download personal knowledge data.

Delete account data.

14. Security Experience

Security features include:

Account protection.

Authentication management.

Session management.

Security should remain simple for students.

15. Notification Settings

Students control:

Learning Notifications

Examples:

Preparation reminders.

Revision suggestions.

Academic Notifications

Examples:

Upcoming assessments.

Study milestones.

Community Notifications

Examples:

Study group activities.

16. Offline Account Experience

Offline mode allows access to:

Cached profile information.

Local preferences.

Downloaded learning content.

Account changes require synchronization.

17. Account Lifecycle

States:

New Account

Initial setup.

Active Student

Regular learning usage.

Subscription Expired

Limited access state.

Account Archived

Historical learning data retained according to policy.

18. Architecture Freeze

The Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience establishes the student's control layer.

The system shall:

Give students ownership of their learning identity.

Support direct student subscription.

Enable individual and group plans.

Provide AI personalisation controls.

Maintain privacy and transparency.

Support long-term student relationships.

The profile layer ensures AI Study Buddy remains a trusted personal learning companion.
