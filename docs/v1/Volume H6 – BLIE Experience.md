# AI Study Buddy

# Volume H6 – BLIE Experience

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The BLIE Experience defines how students interact with the Buddy Learning Intelligent Engine.

BLIE is the intelligence layer that helps students:

- Understand concepts.
- Prepare before learning.
- Clarify confusion.
- Connect knowledge.
- Improve mastery.

BLIE transforms AI Study Buddy from a learning application into a personal educational companion.

---

# 2. BLIE Experience Philosophy

BLIE follows these principles.

## Learning Companion, Not Chatbot

BLIE should feel like a knowledgeable study partner.

The objective is not answering questions only.

The objective is improving understanding.

---

## Context-Aware Intelligence

BLIE understands:

- Current subject.
- Current topic.
- Student knowledge level.
- Previous learning history.

---

## Guided Learning

BLIE should guide students toward understanding rather than simply providing answers.

---

## Student Growth Focus

Every interaction should contribute to learning progress.

---

# 3. BLIE Role in Student Journey

BLIE supports four learning stages.

---

## Before Learning

Purpose:

Prepare the student.

Examples:

- Explain upcoming concepts.
- Identify prerequisites.
- Provide background knowledge.

---

## During Learning

Purpose:

Support understanding.

Examples:

- Explain difficult concepts.
- Answer questions.
- Connect ideas.

---

## After Learning

Purpose:

Strengthen memory.

Examples:

- Summarise topics.
- Generate revision materials.
- Test understanding.

---

## Before Assessment

Purpose:

Build confidence.

Examples:

- Practice questions.
- Identify weak areas.
- Review important concepts.

# 8. BLIE Learning Guidance

BLIE should proactively support learning.

Examples:

"You are starting Algorithms next week. Would you like to review prerequisite concepts?"

"You struggled with recursion previously. Would you like a quick revision?"

---

# 9. BLIE and PLKG Integration

BLIE uses the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

The PLKG helps BLIE understand:

- What the student knows.
- What concepts are related.
- What foundations may be missing.

Example:

Student asks:

"Explain neural networks."

BLIE understands whether the student already understands:

- Linear algebra.
- Probability.
- Machine learning basics.

---

# 10. BLIE Memory Experience

BLIE should provide continuity.

The student should feel:

"You remember my learning journey."

Memory may include:

- Previous discussions.
- Learning progress.
- Preferred explanations.
- Knowledge gaps.

Memory must always respect privacy controls.

---

# 11. BLIE Learning Actions

BLIE may suggest:

- Study preparation.

# 12. BLIE Trust Experience

# 15. Voice Interaction Readiness

The BLIE experience should support future voice interaction.

Design considerations:

- Natural conversation.
- Spoken explanations.
- Accessibility.

---

# 16. Responsible AI Principles

BLIE shall:

- Avoid pretending certainty.
- Encourage understanding.
- Protect student privacy.
- Support academic integrity.

BLIE helps students learn; it does not replace learning effort.

---

# 17. Architecture Freeze

The BLIE Experience establishes how students interact with AI Study Buddy intelligence.

BLIE shall:

1. Act as a learning companion.
2. Understand student context.
3. Support preparation, learning, revision, and assessment.
4. Use PLKG to personalise guidance.
5. Build long-term learning continuity.
6. Maintain trust and responsible AI behaviour.

BLIE is the bridge between artificial intelligence and the student's personal learning journey.
