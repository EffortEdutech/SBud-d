# AI Study Buddy

## Volume A – Product Vision & Strategy

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

AI Study Buddy is a personal AI learning companion designed to help university students succeed throughout their academic journey. It accompanies students from their first semester until graduation by understanding their courses, building a personalized knowledge graph, and providing intelligent guidance before, during, and after every learning experience.

The application is built for individual students and will be distributed through the Google Play Store, Apple App Store, and the Web. Students subscribe directly to the service without requiring any integration with their university.

---

# 2. Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI Study Buddy, empowering every university student with a lifelong academic companion that helps them learn smarter, remember better, and graduate with confidence.

---

# 3. Mission

Our mission is to transform the way students learn by combining artificial intelligence with a continuously evolving personal knowledge graph.

Rather than simply answering questions, AI Study Buddy prepares students before class, supports them during learning, and reinforces their understanding after class. Every interaction helps the AI understand the student's learning journey more deeply.

---

# 4. Target Users

AI Study Buddy is designed for:

* Undergraduate students
* Diploma students
* Postgraduate students
* Students preparing for professional certification

The product focuses exclusively on individual learners (B2C).

---

# 5. Product Philosophy

The platform is guided by six principles:

**Student First**
Every feature exists to improve the student's learning experience.

**Learning Before Answers**
The AI encourages understanding instead of providing shortcuts.

**Memory Creates Better Learning**
Long-term educational memory enables personalized guidance.

**Preparation Builds Confidence**
Students should understand prerequisite concepts before new topics begin.

**Knowledge is Connected**
Learning is a network of related concepts, not isolated documents.

**Growth Never Stops**
The companion grows with the student throughout university, continuously strengthening its understanding of the learner.

---

# 6. Core Innovation

Every student owns a **Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG)**.

The graph is created immediately after the student enters their university programme, semester, and enrolled subjects.

BLIE builds an initial knowledge structure based on the curriculum and expands it continuously as the student uploads lecture notes, asks questions, completes assignments, takes quizzes, creates notes, and studies new concepts.

Instead of managing files, AI Study Buddy manages knowledge.

---

# 7. Buddy Learning Intelligent Engine (BLIE)

AI Study Buddy is powered by the **Buddy Learning Intelligent Engine (BLIE)**.

BLIE is the intelligence core responsible for:

* Building and maintaining the Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG)
* Understanding subject relationships and prerequisite concepts
* Preparing students before new topics begin
* Assisting students during learning
* Reinforcing understanding through revision and practice
* Continuously updating the student's academic memory

BLIE becomes more intelligent as the student's knowledge graph evolves semester by semester.

---

# 8. Learning Model

AI Study Buddy supports learning through three continuous phases.

### Before Learning

BLIE analyses the student's subjects and identifies prerequisite knowledge before each new topic begins. It creates personalized preparation plans so students enter class with stronger foundational understanding.

### During Learning

Students upload lecture slides, PDFs, images, personal notes, and interact with the AI. BLIE maps every new piece of information into the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

### After Learning

BLIE generates summaries, flashcards, quizzes, revision plans, and identifies knowledge gaps to strengthen long-term understanding and exam readiness.

---

Revenue streams include:

* Free Plan
* Premium Subscription

* Educational Marketplace (Future Version)

The application is available through:

* Google Play Store

---

# 10. Product Goal

The goal of AI Study Buddy is not to replace lecturers or independent study.

Its purpose is to become every student's trusted academic companion by preserving knowledge, strengthening understanding, and providing intelligent guidance throughout the entire university journey.

By graduation, students will possess not only a degree but also a complete, structured Personal Learning Knowledge Graph that represents everything they have learned, understood, and achieved.

---

# 11. Architecture Freeze

The following principles are frozen for Version 1:

* AI Study Buddy is a B2C product for individual university students.
* The platform is distributed through app stores and the web.
* Every student owns a Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG).
* BLIE is the sole intelligence engine powering the platform.
* BLIE prepares students before learning, guides them during learning, and reinforces learning afterward.
* All features must directly support the student's personal learning journey.

These principles define the foundation of AI Study Buddy Version 1 and shall remain unchanged unless formally revised.
