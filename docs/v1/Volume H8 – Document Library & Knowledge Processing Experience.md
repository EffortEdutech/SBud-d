# Volume H8 – Document Library & Knowledge Processing Experience

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

# 1. Introduction

The Document Library & Knowledge Processing Experience defines how students manage their academic materials and how AI Study Buddy transforms those materials into structured learning knowledge.

Students commonly receive learning materials in many forms:

- Lecture notes.
- PDF slides.
- Images.
- Handwritten notes.
- Reference documents.
- Study materials.

AI Study Buddy converts these disconnected materials into organised knowledge connected to the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG).

---

# 2. Document Experience Philosophy

The document experience follows these principles.

## Knowledge Over Storage

The purpose is not to become a cloud drive.

The purpose is to create understanding.

---

## Simple Capture

Students should easily capture learning materials.

---

## Intelligent Processing

AI handles the complexity of organising information.

---

## Student Ownership

The student's learning materials and generated knowledge remain part of their personal learning journey.

# 18. Trust and Transparency

Students should understand:

- What AI extracted.
- How knowledge was created.
- Which source document generated the information.

AI-generated knowledge should always maintain connection to learning sources.

---

# 19. Architecture Freeze

The Document Library & Knowledge Processing Experience establishes how student materials become personal learning intelligence.

The system shall:

1. Make uploading simple.
2. Transform documents into structured knowledge.
3. Connect extracted knowledge to PLKG.
4. Support BLIE contextual learning.
5. Preserve student ownership.
6. Maintain transparency between source materials and generated knowledge.

Documents are not the final product.

Knowledge growth is the final product.
