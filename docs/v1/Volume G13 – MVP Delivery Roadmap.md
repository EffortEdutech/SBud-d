# AI Study Buddy

# Volume G13 – MVP Delivery Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The MVP Delivery Roadmap defines the implementation sequence for delivering the first public version of AI Study Buddy.

The objective of the MVP is to validate the platform's core mission:

**To become a trusted AI learning companion that helps university students learn more effectively throughout their academic journey.**

The MVP focuses on delivering a complete and reliable learning experience rather than a large feature set.

---

# 2. MVP Objectives

The MVP shall demonstrate that AI Study Buddy can:

- Support individual university students.
- Build and maintain a Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG).
- Provide personalized learning through BLIE.
- Process student learning materials.
- Support offline learning.
- Synchronize seamlessly with cloud services.

The MVP establishes the foundation for future platform growth.

---

# 3. MVP Delivery Principles

Implementation follows these principles.

## Foundation First

Core platform capabilities shall be completed before advanced features.

---

## Vertical Delivery

Each capability shall be implemented from frontend through backend to deployment before beginning the next capability.

---

## Quality Before Expansion

Platform stability is prioritised over feature quantity.

---

## Student Validation

Real student feedback guides future improvements after MVP release.

# 12. MVP Release

The MVP is released when:

- Core learning workflows operate correctly.
- BLIE provides reliable educational assistance.
- PLKG functions correctly.
- Document Intelligence operates reliably.
- Synchronization is stable.
- Security requirements are satisfied.

The release represents the first complete implementation of AI Study Buddy.

---

# 13. Post-MVP Evaluation

Following release, the engineering team shall evaluate:

- Student adoption.
- Learning effectiveness.
- Platform stability.
- Operational performance.
- Student feedback.

Evaluation informs future development while preserving the approved architecture.

---

# 14. Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful when:

- Students can complete their learning journey using the platform.
- BLIE consistently provides useful educational guidance.
- The PLKG grows correctly with student learning.
- Learning materials are processed successfully.
- Offline learning operates reliably.
- Students experience a stable and intuitive application.

---

# 15. Transition Beyond MVP

After successful MVP validation, future development shall expand the platform through the approved architecture without changing its core mission.

Future enhancements shall continue to support:

- Personalized learning.
- Long-term knowledge growth.
- Educational intelligence.
- Student success.

Expansion shall remain consistent with the Engineering Constitution.

---

# 16. Architecture Freeze

The MVP Delivery Roadmap establishes the implementation sequence for the first public release of AI Study Buddy.

The platform shall:

1. Deliver the approved core learning experience.
2. Validate BLIE and PLKG in real-world student usage.
3. Prioritize stability, quality, and educational value.
4. Release only after successful technical and functional validation.
5. Use student feedback to guide future iterations while preserving the approved architecture.

The MVP represents the beginning of the AI Study Buddy journey—not the completion of the platform.

---

# Implementation Notes (Not Part of Current Architecture)

Future delivery plans may introduce additional phases and capabilities after the MVP, provided they remain aligned with the approved architecture and Engineering Constitution.
