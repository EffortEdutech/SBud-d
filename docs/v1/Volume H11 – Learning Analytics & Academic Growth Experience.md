# AI Study Buddy

# Volume H11 – Learning Analytics & Academic Growth Experience

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The Learning Analytics & Academic Growth Experience defines how AI Study Buddy measures, visualises, and communicates student learning progress.

The system transforms learning activity into meaningful insights that help students improve.

Analytics are generated from:

- Learning activities.
- Subject progress.
- PLKG development.
- BLIE interactions.
- Revision activities.
- Assessment preparation.

---

# 2. Analytics Philosophy

The analytics experience follows these principles.

---

## Growth Over Comparison

Students should measure progress against their own learning journey.

The objective is personal improvement.

---

## Understanding Over Numbers

Numbers alone do not represent learning.

Analytics must explain meaning.

---

## Actionable Insights

Every insight should suggest a possible next action.

---

## Student Ownership

Learning analytics belong to the student.

---

# 4. Knowledge Growth Analytics

The system measures PLKG development.

Indicators:

- New concepts acquired.
- Knowledge connections created.
- Topics understood.
- Knowledge gaps reduced.

Example:

"Your Computer Networks knowledge has expanded with 12 connected concepts this month."

---

# 5. Subject Progress Analytics

Each subject provides learning insights.

Displays:

- Topic progress.
- Understanding level.
- Revision needs.
- Preparation status.

Example:

```
Database Systems

Topics:
8 / 12 introduced

Strong:
Normalization

Needs Review:
Transaction Management
```

---

# 6. Mastery Development

Mastery represents confidence and understanding.

Possible states:

## Introduced

Student has encountered the concept.

---

## Learning

Student is developing understanding.

---

## Practicing

Student applies knowledge.

---

## Confident

Student demonstrates strong understanding.

---

## Needs Reinforcement

Concept requires review.

---

# 7. Learning Habit Analytics

The system may analyse learning patterns.

Examples:

- Study consistency.
- Preparation habits.
- Revision frequency.
- Learning sessions.

The purpose is awareness, not judgement.

---

# 8. BLIE Learning Insights

BLIE can provide personalised observations.

Examples:

"You usually understand concepts better after examples. I will adjust explanations."

"You have not reviewed this topic recently. A short revision may help."

---

# 9. Academic Journey Timeline

Students can view their long-term progress.

Timeline:

```
Semester 1

↓

Foundation Knowledge

↓

Semester 2

↓

Connected Concepts

↓

Final Year

↓

Advanced Understanding
```

The student sees growth over time.

---

# 10. Learning Gap Analytics

The system identifies areas requiring attention.

Examples:

- Missing prerequisite knowledge.
- Unclear concepts.
- Weak connections.

The goal is recovery, not failure indication.

---

# 11. Preparation Analytics

The system tracks readiness.

Examples:

Upcoming topic:

"Operating Systems"

Preparation status:

- Required concepts reviewed.
- Background knowledge completed.
- Ready for lecture.

---

# 12. Assessment Readiness

Before examinations, students can view:

- Prepared topics.
- Weak areas.
- Recommended revision.

Example:

"Your strongest topics are A and B. Focus revision on C and D."

---

# 13. Personal Learning Report

Students may generate periodic reports.

Examples:

Weekly:

- Learning activities.
- Progress summary.

Semester:

- Knowledge growth.
- Subject development.

Academic journey:

- Long-term learning history.

---

# 14. Analytics Visualisation Principles

Analytics should be:

## Clear

Easy to understand.

## Motivating

Encourage progress.

## Calm

Avoid creating anxiety.

## Educational

Explain meaning.

---

# 15. Privacy Model

Student analytics are private by default.

Students control:

- Data access.
- Sharing.
- Visibility.

Academic growth data belongs to the student.

---

# 16. Offline Experience

Offline analytics include:

Available:

- Cached progress.
- Previous reports.
- Local learning history.

Pending:

- Cloud-generated insights.
- Updated analytics.

# 18. Architecture Freeze

The Learning Analytics & Academic Growth Experience establishes the reflection system of AI Study Buddy.

The system shall:

1. Show personal learning growth.
2. Explain knowledge development.
3. Identify learning gaps.
4. Support better study decisions.
5. Encourage continuous improvement.
6. Preserve student ownership and privacy.

Analytics transform learning activities into personal academic intelligence.
