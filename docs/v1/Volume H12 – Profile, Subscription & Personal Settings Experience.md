# AI Study Buddy

# Volume H12 – Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience defines how students manage their personal identity, subscription plan, preferences, and privacy controls.

This layer supports the long-term relationship between the student and AI Study Buddy.

The purpose is to provide:

- Control.
- Transparency.
- Personalisation.
- Trust.

---

# 2. Profile Experience Philosophy

The profile experience follows these principles.

---

## Student Ownership

The student's:

- Learning history.
- PLKG.
- Preferences.
- Progress.

belong to the student.

---

## Simple Management

Settings should be understandable and easy to control.

---

## Personalisation

Students can configure how AI Study Buddy supports them.

---

## Trust and Transparency

Students understand:

- What data exists.
- How it is used.
- How they control it.

# 8. Subscription Experience

AI Study Buddy follows a direct-to-student subscription model.

The platform is designed for individual students downloading the application from app stores.

---

# 9. Subscription Models

Initial structure:

## Individual Subscription

Personal AI Study Buddy.

Includes:

- Personal BLIE access.
- Personal PLKG.
- Learning analytics.
- Document intelligence.

---

## Group Subscription

Designed for students learning together.

Benefits:

- Lower cost per student.
- Shared study resources.
- Collaborative learning environment.

# 18. Architecture Freeze

The Profile, Subscription & Personal Settings Experience establishes the student's control layer.

The system shall:

1. Give students ownership of their learning identity.
2. Support direct student subscription.
3. Enable individual and group plans.
4. Provide AI personalisation controls.
5. Maintain privacy and transparency.
6. Support long-term student relationships.

The profile layer ensures AI Study Buddy remains a trusted personal learning companion.
