# AI Study Buddy

# Volume B – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Product Overview

AI Study Buddy is a mobile and web application that helps university students manage, understand, and retain knowledge throughout their academic journey.

The application combines artificial intelligence with a Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG) to create a personalized learning experience that evolves from the student's first semester until graduation.

---

# 2. Product Goal

Enable every student to:

* Prepare before learning.
* Understand during learning.
* Reinforce after learning.
* Build a lifelong academic memory.

---

# 3. Core User Journey

### Step 1 – Onboarding

The student creates an account and enters:

* University (optional)
* Degree Programme
* Current Semester
* Subjects Enrolled

AI Study Buddy initializes the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

---

### Step 2 – Learning Preparation

Before classes begin, BLIE:

* Builds the initial subject knowledge graph.
* Identifies prerequisite concepts.
* Generates a personalized preparation plan.
* Recommends foundational topics to study.

---

### Step 3 – Learning

Throughout the semester, students can:

* Upload lecture PDFs.
* Upload images of notes or whiteboards.
* Create personal notes.
* Chat with AI.
* Ask questions.
* Organize study materials.

BLIE continuously updates the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

---

### Step 4 – Revision

The application automatically assists by generating:

* Study summaries.
* Flashcards.
* Practice quizzes.
* Revision schedules.
* Knowledge gap analysis.
* Exam preparation plans.

---

### Step 5 – Academic Growth

As each semester progresses, AI Study Buddy expands the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph, creating a structured academic memory that connects concepts across subjects and semesters.

# 6. Key Product Principles

AI Study Buddy shall:

* Encourage understanding instead of shortcut answers.
* Build knowledge progressively.
* Preserve long-term academic memory.
* Personalize every student's learning journey.
* Continuously improve through interaction.

---

# 7. Success Criteria

A successful AI Study Buddy enables students to:

* Arrive prepared for lectures.
* Learn more effectively.
* Revise more efficiently.
* Retain knowledge longer.
* Build confidence throughout university.
* Graduate with a complete Personal Learning Knowledge Graph representing their academic journey.
