# AI Study Buddy

# Volume E – Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG)

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The **Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG)** is the digital representation of a student's academic knowledge.

Rather than storing learning materials as isolated files, the PLKG organizes knowledge into connected concepts, allowing AI Study Buddy to understand not only what a student has learned, but also how different ideas relate to one another.

Every student owns a private PLKG that grows continuously from the first semester until graduation.

---

# 2. Vision

To create a living academic memory that reflects each student's unique learning journey and becomes increasingly valuable throughout their university life.

---

# 3. Purpose

The PLKG exists to:

* Organize knowledge instead of files.
* Connect related concepts across subjects.
* Preserve long-term academic memory.
* Help students understand relationships between topics.
* Enable BLIE to provide personalized learning guidance.

---

# 4. Knowledge Foundation

The PLKG begins as soon as the student completes onboarding.

Using the student's:

* Degree programme
* Current semester
* Enrolled subjects

BLIE creates an initial knowledge structure based on the curriculum and expected learning outcomes.

This provides the foundation upon which all future learning is built.

---

# 5. Knowledge Growth

As the student learns, the PLKG continuously expands through:

* Lecture notes
* PDFs
* Images
* Personal notes
* AI conversations
* Assignments
* Practice quizzes
* Flashcards
* Revision activities

Each new learning experience strengthens and enriches the student's knowledge graph.

---

# 6. Connected Learning

Knowledge within the PLKG is interconnected.

Concepts are linked to:

* Related concepts
* Subjects
* Topics
* Learning materials
* Student notes
* AI explanations
* Practice activities

These connections help students understand how knowledge builds across different subjects and semesters.

---

# 7. Knowledge Evolution

The PLKG evolves alongside the student.

As understanding improves, the graph becomes richer, more detailed, and more interconnected.

The graph is never static.

It reflects the student's ongoing academic development throughout university.

---

# 8. Learning Support

BLIE uses the PLKG to:

* Recommend prerequisite concepts.
* Explain difficult topics.
* Identify knowledge gaps.
* Personalize revision.
* Generate relevant practice questions.
* Guide future learning.

Every recommendation is informed by the student's own learning history.

---

# 9. Student Ownership

The Personal Learning Knowledge Graph belongs to the student.

It represents their individual learning journey, academic progress, and accumulated understanding.

The graph is private and exists to support the student's personal growth.

---

# 10. Product Value

By graduation, the student possesses more than lecture notes and completed assignments.

They own a structured academic knowledge graph that captures years of learning, connections between concepts, and intellectual growth.

This becomes a lasting educational asset that reflects their unique university experience.

---

# Architecture Freeze

The Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG) is the central knowledge model of AI Study Buddy.

BLIE continuously builds, maintains, and reasons over the PLKG to deliver personalized learning support before, during, and after every stage of the student's academic journey.

Every feature within AI Study Buddy shall strengthen, enrich, or utilize the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.
