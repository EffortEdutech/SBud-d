# Volume H9 – Study Preparation & Revision Experience

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

# 1. Introduction

The Study Preparation & Revision Experience defines how AI Study Buddy helps students prepare before learning and strengthen understanding after learning.

The objective is to move students from passive learning to active knowledge development.

The experience supports the complete learning cycle:

```
Prepare

↓

Learn

↓

Understand

↓

Practice

↓

Review

↓

Master
```

---

# 2. Study Preparation Philosophy

The study preparation experience follows these principles.

---

## Preparation Before Learning

Students should not enter a topic without context.

AI Study Buddy helps students build the required foundation before lectures begin.

---

## Understanding Before Memorisation

The system prioritises conceptual understanding.

---

## Continuous Reinforcement

Knowledge is strengthened through repeated meaningful interaction.

---

## Personalised Learning Path

Each student receives guidance based on their PLKG.

---

# 4. Study Preparation Experience

Study Preparation helps students before a topic begins.

Example:

Upcoming lecture:

"Machine Learning - Neural Networks"

BLIE prepares:

Required foundations:

- Linear algebra.
- Functions.
- Basic probability.

Preparation activities:

- Concept introduction.
- Short explanations.
- Related knowledge review.

---

# 5. Pre-Lecture Preparation

Before attending a lecture, students may receive:

- Topic overview.
- Key terminology.
- Required background knowledge.
- Expected learning outcomes.

The student arrives prepared.

---

# 6. Topic Readiness Indicator

Each topic may have a readiness state.

Examples:

## Not Started

Student has not prepared.

---

## Preparing

Student is building foundation.

---

## Ready

Student has required background knowledge.

---

## Needs Support

Student requires additional assistance.

---

# 7. BLIE Study Preparation Role

BLIE acts as a learning coach.

Examples:

"Your next topic requires understanding of derivatives. Would you like a quick review?"

"You completed preparation. Let's test your understanding."

---

# 8. Revision Experience

Revision focuses on strengthening memory.

Students can:

- Review previous topics.
- Identify weak concepts.
- Ask BLIE for explanations.
- Practice understanding.

---

# 9. Personalised Revision

Revision is generated from:

- PLKG status.
- Previous learning activity.
- Assessment schedule.
- Knowledge gaps.

Different students receive different revision paths.

---

# 10. Knowledge Gap Recovery

The system identifies missing foundations.

Example:

Student struggles with:

Advanced Programming

System identifies:

Needs reinforcement:

- Variables.
- Functions.
- Data structures.

The student receives targeted recovery guidance.

---

# 11. Practice Experience

Practice activities may include:

- Concept questions.
- Explanations.
- Examples.
- Problem-solving exercises.

The objective is understanding.

---

# 12. Assessment Preparation

Before exams, students receive structured preparation.

Support includes:

- Important concept review.
- Topic prioritisation.
- Practice sessions.
- Weak area identification.

---

# 13. Revision Timeline

Students can view:

- Upcoming assessments.
- Recommended revision.
- Completed preparation.
- Remaining knowledge gaps.

---

# 15. Learning Reflection

Students may reflect on:

- What they understood.
- What remains unclear.
- What requires review.

Reflection strengthens learning awareness.

---

# 16. Gamification Philosophy

Motivation is based on meaningful learning progress.

Examples:

- Topic mastery.
- Knowledge growth.
- Preparation completion.
- Consistent learning habits.

The system avoids rewarding unnecessary usage.

---

# 17. Offline Experience

Offline study support includes:

Pending:

- New AI-generated recommendations.
- Cloud updates.

# 19. Architecture Freeze

The Study Preparation & Revision Experience establishes AI Study Buddy as a proactive learning companion.

The system shall:

1. Prepare students before topics begin.
2. Use PLKG to personalise learning.
3. Support continuous revision.
4. Identify and recover knowledge gaps.
5. Assist assessment preparation.
6. Encourage meaningful academic progress.

AI Study Buddy does not wait for students to struggle.

It helps them become ready.
