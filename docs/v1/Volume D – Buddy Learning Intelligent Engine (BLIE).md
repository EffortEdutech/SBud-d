# AI Study Buddy

# Volume D – Buddy Learning Intelligent Engine (BLIE)

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The **Buddy Learning Intelligent Engine (BLIE)** is the intelligence core of AI Study Buddy.

BLIE is not a general-purpose chatbot. It is a specialized learning engine designed to understand each student's academic journey, continuously build their Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG), and provide personalized guidance throughout university.

Every AI capability within AI Study Buddy is powered by BLIE.

---

# 2. Vision

To become every student's personal learning intelligence that understands what they know, identifies what they need to learn next, and guides them towards academic success.

---

# 3. Mission

BLIE exists to transform educational information into structured knowledge and convert that knowledge into personalized learning guidance.

Rather than simply answering questions, BLIE continuously helps students prepare, understand, revise, and retain knowledge.

---

# 4. Core Responsibilities

BLIE is responsible for:

* Building the Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG).
* Understanding relationships between subjects and concepts.
* Preparing students before new topics begin.
* Assisting students during learning.
* Reinforcing knowledge after learning.
* Tracking academic growth across semesters.
* Personalizing every learning recommendation.

---

# 5. Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG)

Every student owns a private Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

The graph is initialized when the student enters:

* Degree Programme
* Current Semester
* Enrolled Subjects

BLIE creates the initial knowledge structure based on the curriculum and continuously expands it as the student's learning progresses.

The PLKG becomes the student's lifelong academic memory.

---

# 6. Learning Lifecycle

BLIE supports three continuous learning phases.

### Before Learning

BLIE:

* Analyses the syllabus.
* Identifies prerequisite concepts.
* Recommends preparation activities.
* Builds confidence before lectures.

---

### During Learning

BLIE:

* Understands uploaded PDFs and images.
* Organizes lecture materials.
* Maps new knowledge into the PLKG.
* Explains concepts through personalized guidance.

---

### After Learning

BLIE:

* Generates summaries.
* Creates flashcards.
* Produces practice quizzes.
* Detects knowledge gaps.
* Plans revision activities.

---

# 7. Intelligence Principles

BLIE follows six guiding principles.

### Personal

Every recommendation is tailored to the individual student's learning journey.

### Contextual

Responses are based on the student's own knowledge graph rather than isolated conversations.

### Proactive

BLIE prepares students before difficulties arise.

### Adaptive

Learning recommendations evolve as understanding improves.

### Connected

Knowledge is treated as an interconnected network of concepts.

### Continuous

The learning journey extends across every semester until graduation.

---

# 8. Inputs

BLIE learns from:

* Degree programme
* Semester structure
* Subject syllabus
* Lecture notes
* PDFs
* Images
* Student notes
* AI conversations
* Assignments
* Quizzes
* Flashcards

Every interaction enriches the Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.

---

# 9. Outputs

BLIE provides:

* Personalized explanations
* Study preparation plans
* Concept relationships
* Learning summaries
* Flashcards
* Practice quizzes
* Revision schedules
* Knowledge gap analysis
* Learning recommendations

---

# 10. Product Goal

BLIE is designed to become the student's trusted learning companion.

Its success is measured not by the number of questions answered, but by the student's growth in understanding, confidence, and academic achievement.

As the student progresses from Semester 1 to graduation, BLIE continuously grows alongside them, making every semester more personalized than the last.

---

# Architecture Freeze

For Version 1, BLIE is defined as the single intelligence engine powering AI Study Buddy.

Its primary responsibility is to build, maintain, and reason over each student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph while supporting learning before, during, and after every stage of the university journey.

All future AI capabilities shall align with this mission.
