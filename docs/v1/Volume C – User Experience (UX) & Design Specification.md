# AI Study Buddy

# Volume C – User Experience (UX) & Design Specification

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Purpose

The User Experience of AI Study Buddy is designed to make learning simple, personal, and engaging.

The application should feel like a trusted study companion rather than a traditional educational application. Every interaction should reduce learning complexity, encourage curiosity, and help students build knowledge with confidence.

---

# 2. Design Principles

The user experience is guided by the following principles:

* **Student First** – Every screen should help students learn more effectively.
* **Simple by Default** – Present only what is needed and avoid unnecessary complexity.
* **Knowledge Before Files** – Emphasize concepts and connections rather than document storage.
* **AI Everywhere** – BLIE should be available throughout the application to provide guidance and support.
* **Progress is Visible** – Students should clearly see their learning growth over time.

---

# 3. Primary Navigation

The application consists of six main sections:

### Home

A personalized dashboard showing:

* Daily study plan
* Upcoming classes
* Learning reminders
* Recent activity
* AI recommendations

---

### Subjects

Displays all enrolled subjects.

Each subject contains:

* Topics
* Lecture materials
* Notes
* Assignments
* Revision resources
* Knowledge map

---

### AI Buddy

The main conversation interface with BLIE.

Students can:

* Ask questions
* Request explanations
* Solve problems
* Explore concepts
* Receive study guidance

---

### Knowledge Graph

Visualizes the student's Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG).

Students can:

* Explore connected concepts
* View learning progress
* Identify weak areas
* Discover prerequisite topics

---

### Revision

A dedicated space for:

* Flashcards
* Practice quizzes
* Summaries
* Revision plans
* Knowledge gap reviews

---

### Profile

Contains:

* Academic profile
* Semester information
* Subscription
* Learning statistics
* Settings

---

# 4. Student Journey

The learning journey follows a continuous cycle:

1. Enroll in semester
2. Add subjects
3. BLIE prepares learning roadmap
4. Attend lectures
5. Upload learning materials
6. Ask AI for guidance
7. Review knowledge graph
8. Revise and practice
9. Complete semester
10. Continue to the next semester

Each semester builds upon the previous one, creating continuous academic growth.

---

# 5. AI Experience

BLIE should always behave like a supportive study companion.

It should:

* Explain clearly.
* Encourage critical thinking.
* Recommend prerequisite learning.
* Identify knowledge gaps.
* Suggest revision strategies.
* Adapt to the student's learning progress.

The AI should guide rather than simply provide answers.

---

# 6. Knowledge Graph Experience

The Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG) is a core part of the user experience.

Students should be able to:

* See how concepts connect.
* Track mastery of topics.
* Explore relationships between subjects.
* Understand prerequisite knowledge.
* Watch their knowledge graph expand throughout their degree.

The graph should make learning visible and meaningful.

---

# 7. Learning Preparation Experience

Before each new topic, BLIE should:

* Introduce the upcoming subject matter.
* Recommend prerequisite concepts.
* Estimate preparation time.
* Suggest learning resources.
* Build student confidence before class.

Preparation should become a natural part of the learning routine.

---

# 8. Revision Experience

After learning, the application should automatically help students retain knowledge through:

* AI-generated summaries
* Flashcards
* Practice questions
* Personalized revision schedules
* Knowledge gap analysis

The goal is to reinforce long-term understanding rather than short-term memorization.

---

# 9. Visual Style

The interface should communicate clarity and focus.

Key characteristics include:

* Clean and modern design
* Minimal visual clutter
* Consistent navigation
* Readable typography
* Clear progress indicators
* Interactive knowledge graph
* Light and Dark modes

Every design decision should reduce cognitive load and help students stay focused.

---

# 10. Success Criteria

A successful user experience enables students to:

* Feel prepared before lectures.
* Learn with confidence during classes.
* Revise efficiently after classes.
* Understand how concepts connect.
* Build a growing Personal Learning Knowledge Graph.
* Experience AI Study Buddy as a trusted academic companion throughout university.

---

# Architecture Freeze

The user experience of AI Study Buddy shall remain centered on the student.

Every screen, workflow, and interaction must strengthen the student's personal learning journey and reinforce the role of BLIE as a supportive learning companion.
