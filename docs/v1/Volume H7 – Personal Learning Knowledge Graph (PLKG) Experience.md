# 1. Introduction

The Personal Learning Knowledge Graph (PLKG) Experience defines how students visualize and interact with their personal learning intelligence.

The PLKG represents the evolution of a student's knowledge throughout their university journey.

It transforms learning from disconnected materials into a connected understanding system.

---

# 2. PLKG Experience Philosophy

The PLKG follows these principles.

## Knowledge Growth, Not Data Display

The purpose is understanding, not showing technical graph complexity.

---

## Personal Knowledge Journey

Every student's PLKG is unique.

It reflects:

- Subjects studied.
- Topics learned.
- Concepts understood.
- Knowledge relationships.

---

## Progressive Discovery

The graph becomes richer as the student learns.

---

## Action-Oriented Understanding

The PLKG should help students decide:

"What should I learn next?"

# 4. PLKG Main Views

The student experience includes several perspectives.

---

## Overview View

Purpose:

Show overall knowledge growth.

Displays:

- Knowledge areas.
- Subject connections.
- Learning progress.

---

## Subject Knowledge View

Purpose:

Explore one subject.

Displays:

- Topics.
- Concepts.
- Relationships.
- Progress.

---

## Topic Detail View

Purpose:

Understand a specific concept.

Displays:

- Related concepts.

---

## Growth Timeline View

Purpose:

Show knowledge evolution.

Displays:

- Semester progression.
- Newly acquired knowledge.
- Major milestones.

# 5. Knowledge Node Experience

A knowledge node represents a learning concept.

A node may contain:

- Topic name.
- Subject relationship.
- Learning status.
- Confidence level.
- Related concepts.

Example:

``` id="n7f4bq"
Machine Learning

Connected to:

├── Linear Algebra

├── Statistics

├── Programming

└── Algorithms
```

# 8. PLKG and BLIE Integration

BLIE uses PLKG information to provide personalised assistance.

Example:

Student asks:

"Explain recursion."

PLKG shows:

- Student knows functions.
- Student struggles with algorithm thinking.

BLIE adjusts the explanation accordingly.

---

# 9. Knowledge Gap Experience

The PLKG helps identify missing foundations.

Example:

Student learning:

Deep Learning

System identifies:

Needs review:

- Matrix operations.
- Probability concepts.

The application recommends preparation.

---

# 10. Learning Path Experience

The PLKG can guide future learning.

Example:

Current:

Programming Basics

↓

Next:

Data Structures

↓

Future:

Artificial Intelligence

The student understands the learning journey.

---

# 11. Visual Design Principles

The PLKG visualization should be:

## Understandable

Students should not need graph knowledge.

---

## Calm

Avoid overwhelming visual complexity.

---

## Interactive

Students can explore relationships naturally.

---

## Educational

Every visual element should communicate learning value.

---

# 12. PLKG Interaction Patterns

Students may:

- Select knowledge nodes.
- Explore relationships.
- Ask BLIE about concepts.
- Review learning history.
- Discover related topics.

---

# 13. Mobile Experience Considerations

Because AI Study Buddy is mobile-first:

The PLKG should support:

- Simplified overview.
- Focused exploration.
- Search-based discovery.
- Topic-centered navigation.

A complete graph view should not overwhelm small screens.

---

# 14. PLKG Growth Experience

Students should feel their knowledge expanding.

Examples:

Semester 1:

Basic foundation.

Semester 3:

Connected knowledge areas.

Final Year:

Complex interdisciplinary understanding.

---

# 15. Privacy and Ownership

The PLKG belongs to the student.

Students should control:

- Their learning history.
- Their personal knowledge data.
- Sharing permissions.

---

# 16. Offline Experience

Offline PLKG support:

Available:

- Cached knowledge.
- Existing relationships.
- Personal progress.

Pending:

- New cloud-generated updates.

# 18. Architecture Freeze

The PLKG Experience establishes the visual and interaction model for personal knowledge growth.

The PLKG shall:

1. Represent the student's personal learning journey.
2. Connect subjects, topics, and concepts.
3. Support BLIE personalisation.
4. Reveal knowledge gaps.
5. Show academic growth over time.
6. Help students understand what to learn next.

The PLKG is the foundation that makes AI Study Buddy a lifelong learning companion rather than a simple study application.
