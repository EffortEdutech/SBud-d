# AI Study Buddy

# Volume F13 – Subscription, Monetization & Usage Control Architecture

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Freeze)

---

# 1. Introduction

The Subscription, Monetization & Usage Control Architecture defines how AI Study Buddy delivers sustainable business growth while remaining accessible to students.

The architecture supports:

* Individual subscriptions.
* Group subscriptions.
* Usage management.
* Storage management.
* AI resource management.
* Future digital learning marketplace.

The objective is to balance affordability for students with long-term platform sustainability.

---

# 2. Vision

To create an affordable AI learning platform where students only pay for additional intelligence, storage, and collaboration features, while maintaining access to essential learning capabilities.

---

# 3. Monetization Principles

The platform follows six principles.

## Student First

Monetization must never reduce educational quality.

---

## Affordable Access

Students should be able to begin learning without financial barriers.

---

## Transparent Usage

Students always understand:

* AI usage.
* Storage consumption.
* Subscription benefits.

---

## Fair Resource Allocation

Heavy AI usage should be managed fairly without affecting other users.

---

## Sustainable Growth

Subscription revenue must support continuous AI and infrastructure improvements.

---

## No Educational Lock-In

Students retain ownership of their learning data regardless of subscription status.

---

# 4. Subscription Architecture

The platform supports multiple plans.

## Free

Designed for:

* New students.
* Trial users.
* Casual learning.

Includes:

* Basic BLIE assistance.
* Limited document uploads.
* Basic PLKG.
* Limited AI interactions.

---

## Premium Individual

Designed for active students.

Additional benefits:

* Higher AI usage.
* Larger storage.
* Expanded PLKG capacity.
* Advanced revision tools.
* Priority AI processing.
* Advanced analytics.

---

## Premium Study Group

Supports collaborative learning.

Benefits include:

* Shared learning repository.
* Group AI tools.
* Collaborative revision.
* Shared storage allocation.
* Lower cost per member.

---

## Future Family Plan

Supports multiple learners within one subscription while maintaining completely separate PLKGs and academic histories.

---

# 5. Usage Control Architecture

Platform resources are measured independently.

Examples:

* AI requests.
* Document processing.
* Storage.
* OCR processing.
* Background analysis.
* Knowledge extraction.

Each resource has configurable limits according to the subscription plan.

---

# 6. AI Usage Management

BLIE intelligently allocates AI resources.

Examples:

Basic requests:

* Short explanations.
* Definitions.
* Quick summaries.

Advanced requests:

* Complex reasoning.
* Large document analysis.
* Personalized study planning.

The AI router selects the most appropriate model to optimise both quality and operational cost.

---

# 7. Storage Architecture

Student storage includes:

* Uploaded documents.
* Images.
* Generated study resources.
* Flashcards.
* Shared materials.

The PLKG itself remains lightweight because it stores structured knowledge rather than entire documents.

---

# 8. Group Subscription Model

Study Groups may subscribe together.

Architecture:

```text id="q8k4rt"
Study Group

↓

Shared Subscription

↓

Shared AI Resources

↓

Individual PLKGs
```

Collaboration reduces subscription costs without merging personal learning intelligence.

---

# 9. Digital Learning Marketplace (Future)

A marketplace may allow trusted creators to publish educational content.

Examples:

* Revision packs.
* Flashcard collections.
* Mind maps.
* Practice examinations.
* Study templates.
* Learning roadmaps.

Students decide what to purchase or download.

---

# 10. Creator Programme (Future)

Verified contributors may publish premium learning resources.

Possible contributors:

* University students.
* Graduates.
* Educators.
* Subject specialists.

BLIE may assist creators by improving formatting, indexing, and concept mapping before publication.

---

# 11. Referral Programme (Future)

Students may invite others to AI Study Buddy.

Rewards may include:

* Temporary Premium access.
* Additional AI usage.
* Extra storage.
* Marketplace credits.

Referral rewards must encourage community growth rather than academic competition.

---

# 12. Usage Dashboard

Students can monitor:

* AI requests used.
* Remaining AI allocation.
* Storage usage.
* Uploaded resources.
* Document processing status.
* Subscription renewal.

Usage information should always be transparent and easy to understand.

---

# 13. Subscription Lifecycle

The platform manages:

* New subscriptions.
* Renewals.
* Upgrades.
* Downgrades.
* Group invitations.
* Payment history.

Changes to subscription status must never compromise ownership of student learning data.

---

# 14. Expired Subscription Policy

If Premium access expires:

Students retain:

* Account.
* PLKG.
* Notes.
* Learning history.
* Uploaded materials.

Premium-only capabilities become unavailable until the subscription is renewed.

Academic memory is never deleted solely because a subscription ends.

---

# 15. Anti-Abuse Controls

The platform monitors:

* Excessive automated usage.
* Shared account abuse.
* Fraudulent subscriptions.
* Automated scraping.
* AI resource abuse.

Protection mechanisms should minimise disruption for legitimate students.

---

# 16. Scalability

The monetization architecture supports:

* Individual subscribers.
* Study Groups.
* Millions of users.
* Multiple currencies.
* Regional pricing.
* Future educational partnerships.

Business expansion does not require changes to the core subscription model.

---

# 17. Future Intelligence

Future enhancements may include:

* AI-powered subscription recommendations.
* Flexible AI credit packages.
* Marketplace revenue sharing.
* University alumni plans.
* Professional learning subscriptions after graduation.

The same subscription architecture can continue supporting learners throughout lifelong education.

---

# Architecture Freeze

The Subscription, Monetization & Usage Control Architecture establishes a sustainable commercial foundation for AI Study Buddy.

The platform shall:

1. Provide meaningful value in the free tier.
2. Offer affordable Premium plans for individuals and Study Groups.
3. Optimise AI usage through intelligent resource allocation.
4. Protect student ownership of learning data.
5. Support future educational marketplaces and creator ecosystems.
6. Scale economically to millions of learners worldwide.

The guiding principle is:

**Students pay for enhanced learning capabilities, never for ownership of their own academic knowledge. BLIE remains a trusted learning companion throughout the student's educational journey.**
